Frozen Yogurt Pops

Category: Dessert/Snack

Makes: 8 Pops

Prep Time: 5 Minutes

Set Time: 4 hours to overnight

This recipe is a revamped throw-back to the
homemade popsicles of my childhood. The
name says it all: frozen yogurt makes a
quick and easy summer-time treat! Adding
some fresh fruit and a popsicle stick
to that equation makes it fun to eat as well!

What You Will Need:

The great thing about this recipe is that there are endless flavour combinations depending on what
you like. Feel free to change up the flavours and fruit combinations to customize your fro-yo pops!

Click on the item once you have it!

From the Fridge:

  • 2 cups Greek yogurt - Any flavour

  •        1.5 cups of fresh fruit:
  • Bananas
  • Kiwi
  • Strawberries
  • Blackberries
  • Blueberries

From the Cupboards:

  • Knife
  • Medium sized mixing bowl
  • Whisk
  • Popsicle Mold
  • Popsicle Sticks

Directions

5 minutes and 5 steps, that's all it takes! Click on the image or the step when you have completed it!

  1. Prepare the fruit: Wash and cut the fruit
    into slices, then place aside for Step 3.
  2. Prepare the yogurt: Pour the yogurt
    into a bowl and whisk until smooth.
  3. Fill the molds: Spoon the yogurt
    into the mold then slide the fruit slices
    into it (place them along, the side
    if you want your pops to look AND
    taste good!    ), continue to spoon yogurt
    and, slide fruit until the mold is filled.
  4. Finish the molds: Slide the sticks down
    the middle of each mold and place
    into the freezer for a minimum
    of 4 hours until overnight.
  5. Prepare to eat: Remove the mold from
    the freezer and run it under warm water
    for a couple of minutes, then gently pull
    and enjoy 1 or 3!!!
